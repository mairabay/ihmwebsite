The Magazines

green. eco. wellness

local. news. events

Our vision is a world where everyone thrives at being In Harmony with their inner and outer Nature.
We see the world as a tapestry of local communities and our vision is to publish local magazines for every community around the world.

Our mission is therefore to become the main source of information in each local community for healthy, eco/green living, working, doing business, etc.
We aim to be a news aggregator for each community on all things healthy/green/eco happening in our community, as well as publishing advice from local professionals in the area, and a calendar of events.

Our ultimate goal is to be the world's largest source of news and education about healthy eating, healthy living, making your home greener, etc.

The Story

by our founder

Maira Bay de Souza

The idea for the In Harmony Magazines came to me in 2015. While checking Social Media, I found information about local upcoming events in the green, eco and wellness sectors. I decided to add them to a calendar, to make it easier to remember the dates, locations, etc. Then, I thought it might be useful to make the calendar public, to share that information with others as well.

From there, the idea was expanded to include news, informational and educational articles. The thought of keeping it local came after noticing that there were no publications dedicated to local events and news, focused on the green, eco and wellness sectors for small regions in Ontario. It also matches the vision of "keeping it local" that I hold with my social enterprise (the Ten Percent Company).
